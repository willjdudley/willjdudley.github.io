Personal Games
Here are a few games I have worked on in my free time.

Speed Punk - Initially started as an Overwatch fan game, this is a simple endless runner that I developed in my free time. It can be found on the Google Play Store.
Romantic Getaway - Winner of the King Game Jam, in Romantic Getaway you must escape from the police while having a heated discussion with your partner.
SIX! The Cricket Game is an alarmingly simple cricket simulator. The intent of this project was to build a shell and structure for a game which could be reused for other projects. It was developed without knowing anything about cricket.
The Red Machine. This was my first solo Unity project. I created a simple arcade shooter while learning the basics of Javascript.
