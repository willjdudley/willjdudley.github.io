Cyber Punk Noodle Bar
This project started as a Blade Runner homage. I didn't want to recreate something directly from the film, but to create something that I felt would fit nicely in the Blade Runner universe. This piece also comes with some heavy influence from Sleeping Dogs. Lighting was a significant challenge I faced when creating this piece. I wanted to portray the messy neon glow that the film is famous for, but kept ending up with an overpowering colour pallet. I think the look of the final piece shows off this look well, without the coloured lighting detracting from the rest of the piece.
